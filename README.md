### About me

Close to 4 years of experience shipping production software across service-based and product-based companies.

Started at Tropsphere Solutions where I built multi-tenant SaaS applications, a custom CRM and booking service for a health tech startup, and worked extensively with architectural patterns like DDD, CQRS, and microservices. That foundation in clean architecture and scalable backend systems is what I carry into everything I build today.

For the past two years at Nbyula, I've been working across the full stack while leading the company's AI engineering efforts from the ground up. What started as the first intelligent agent eventually grew into a dedicated Unified AI Service (UAS) powering multiple agents in production, including voice AI agents handling real inbound and outbound calls.

**Architecture patterns I work with:** DDD, CQRS, Microservices, Event-Driven, Clean Architecture, Hexagonal

If you are working on agentic AI, voice agents, or anything at the intersection of full stack and AI, feel free to reach out.
